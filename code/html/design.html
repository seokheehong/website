Graphic Design

After coding a program that recolored images, I thought, 'This would look really nice if I can make a postcard or a poster out of it.' Research told me that the process I needed to practice was 'vectorizing images.' After a few weeks of planning and convincing my parents to buy Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator (they ended up buying me the entire suite, which was pretty sweet), I finally began my journey as a graphic artist.

The pictures on the left were the images I used as a base, which then I started drawing similar shapes and patterns to match the image.
